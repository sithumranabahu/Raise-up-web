Post Title - Covid-19

Posted on September 23, 2020 at 01:00 PM

The COVID‑19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic, is an ongoing pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‑19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS‑CoV‑2). The disease was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, China.[4] The World Health Organization declared the outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern on 30 January 2020 and a pandemic on 11 March 2020. As of 22 September 2020, more than 31.4 million cases have been reported in 188 countries and territories, resulting in more than 967,000 deaths; more than 21.5 million people have recovered.
